About Us

The Cardiology Interest Group of Jerusalem (CARDIG-Jerusalem) is a student organization under the Faculty of Medicine at Al-Quds University, proudly affiliated with the American College of Cardiology (ACC). Our headquarters are located at Al-Quds University, where we strive to advance cardiovascular education, research, and community outreach.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

We are dedicated to advancing the study and practice of cardiovascular medicine through education, training, and research. We nurture leadership among medical students, elevate community health through outreach, and uphold the highest ethical standards in medicine.

Our Vision

We aspire to be the leading center for cardiovascular scholarship at Al-Quds University, cultivating skilled and compassionate cardiologists who will advance medical knowledge and public welfare in Palestine and beyond.
