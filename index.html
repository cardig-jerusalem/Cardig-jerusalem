About Us

Founded under the Faculty of Medicine at Al-Quds University and affiliated with the American College of Cardiology, CARDIG-Jerusalem is dedicated to advancing cardiovascular education, research, clinical practice, leadership, and community service. Our mission is rooted in excellence and ethical medical practice.

  • Supervising Council & Faculty Advisor

Our threefold hierarchy ensures effective leadership and collaboration across all initiatives.

Mission & Vision

Mission: Advance the study and practice of cardiovascular medicine while upholding ethical medical standards.

Vision: Become a leading hub for cardiovascular scholarship and global collaboration.

Key Objectives

  • Host 4+ seminars and 2 symposia annually
  • Conduct 3+ community service initiatives per semester
  • Achieve 10% annual membership growth
